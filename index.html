About us | Why Fonteyn?

Letting everyone enjoy the outdoors more, that is what we at Fonteyn strive for every day. We achieve this, among other things, by offering you the largest range of outdoor items in Europe. This range focuses on seven specialities: garden furniture, verandas, spas, outdoor kitchens, swimming pools, saunas and garden houses. This complete range is widely displayed in our attractive showroom of no less than 35,000 m2. The articles are also presented to you in attractive garden settings, so that you can gain as much inspiration as possible for your own garden.
